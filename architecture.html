Pipeline Flow

1 — Ingest

Four n8n agent workflows handle dynamic pricing, consumer purchases, restocking, and sellout logging. Live store purchases directly influence agent behavior.

2 — Store

Supabase captures transactions, weather impact, events, products, visit dates, and warehouse activity as the raw source layer.

3 — Transform & Model

Fabric pulls data on a real-time schedule, shapes it into dimensional and fact models, then writes to Warehouse (live) and Lake (daily backup).

4 — Analyze & Act

Power BI dashboards and AI-assisted analysis drive schedule optimization and pricing-agent tuning inside critic-enforced fairness boundaries.

Current Status

Working Now

Agent cadence is tuned to maximize monthly token utilization without hitting limits, with priority on consumer and sellout workflows.
